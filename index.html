Our Selection Rationale

The Akoola System Needs Someone Who Gets the Whole Funnel

Most agencies hand off leads and call it a day. Akoola doesn't. You built a complete client acquisition machine — content that builds trust, ads that create demand, and a US-based call center that contacts every lead within 60 seconds. That last mile is what separates you. But it only works if the top of the funnel is doing its job.

The person running your Meta accounts needs to understand that a lead isn't a win — a booked appointment is. That means optimizing for quality over volume. It means knowing which creatives pull real home service buyers, not tire-kickers. It means managing $100K+ ad spend without letting CPA creep, creative fatigue, or audience saturation eat into your clients' margins.

We looked for candidates with deep Meta Ads execution experience, proven ability to manage and scale high-budget accounts, strong analytical instincts (ROAS, CPA, LTV — not just CTR), and at least some exposure to local/service-based lead generation. We also looked for people who bring structured A/B testing frameworks rather than guesswork — because in Akoola's model, every variable counts.

A note on Leandro: We pre-shortlisted Leandro independently. He is the only candidate in this selection with direct home improvement and roofing experience on Meta Ads — the exact niche Akoola serves. His profile is marked accordingly. We believe the rest of this selection is also strong — but Leandro's niche alignment is hard to ignore.
